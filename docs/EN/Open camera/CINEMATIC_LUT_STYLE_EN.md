# 🎬 SPIRIT OF TENGRI CINEMATIC LUT STYLE

**(documentary + ethnic + poetic realism)**

## 🌄 1. VISUAL IDEA

The style is built on three layers:
* 🌾 steppe air (dust, light, space)
* 🔥 vibrant scene contrast (stage / crowd / sky)
* 🧿 mystical warmth of skin and costumes
👉 feel:
> "reality + light legend"

## 🎨 2. COLOR MODEL (LUT LOGIC)

### 🌤 BASE IMAGE (must be original):
* slightly underexposed
* no blown highlights
* with soft WB (5200–6000K)

### 🌈 COLOR SHIFT (main magic)
**🟠 Shadows → cool**
* shift to teal/blue
* slight cyan in shadows

**🟡 Lights → warm**
* golden/amber
* soft yellow-orange
👉 classic:
> "teal shadows + warm highlights"

### 🧠 CONTRAST
* not "hard"
* but a soft S-curve
👉 goal:
* preserve air
* don't kill details in the steppe and sky

## 🧩 3. SHOTCUT — SPECIFIC SETTINGS

### 🎞 FILTER STACK (in order)
**1. Exposure / Brightness**
* Exposure: -0.10 … -0.25
* Gamma: slightly lower (-0.05)
👉 removes digital "flatness"

**2. Contrast**
* +10 … +18
👉 creates drama in the scene

**3. Saturation**
* -5 to +5 (DO NOT overdo it)
👉 Important: OPPO already produces color — don't break it.

**4. White Balance (if available)**
* Temperature: +200 … +600 (warmer)
* Tint: slightly magenta (+2 … +5)

**5. Shadows / Highlights**
* Shadows: -10
* Highlights: -15 … -25
👉 this creates a "film-like" look.

**6. Color grading (if using)**
**Shadows:**
Hue → teal (190–210°)
Saturation: low
**Midtones:**
neutral / slightly warm
**Highlights:**
amber / gold

## 🌫 4. “STEPPEAK DUST” (SECRET DETAIL)

Add a subtle effect:
* Grain (Film Grain): 5–12%
👉 This simulates:
* air
* festival dust
* vibrant texture

## 🌌 5. PICTURE DEPTH

### ✨ Vignette
very weak (-10 … -15)
👉 focus on the center of the scene (performers / movement)

### 🌫 Sharpness
* don't increase too much
* better slightly down (-5)
👉 removes "digital sharpness"

## 🔥 6. LIGHTING PHILOSOPHY

### You always shoot:
* backlight (sun behind the subject)
* sidelight (golden hour)
* stage lighting (festival evening)
👉 rule:
> "if there is no light, look for a silhouette"

## 🎥 7. EDITING RHYTHM (IMPORTANT FOR ATMOSPHERE)

* long takes (3-8 sec)
* minimum abrupt cuts
* more "observation", less "editing"

## 🧿 8. EMOTIONAL SIGNATURE OF THE STYLE

This LUT style should convey the feeling of:
* wind in the steppe
* the sound of a drum in the distance
* fabric movement on stage
* sun through dust

## 🧪 9. FAST PRESET (if you just want to remember)

```txt
Exposure: -0.2
Contrast: +15
Saturation: 0
Shadows: -10
Highlights: -20
Grain: 8%
White balance: +300K warm
Tone: teal shadows / gold highlights
```

## 💡 10. WHAT DOES THIS GIVE YOU

You don't get a "beautiful video," but:
* a sense of presence
* documentary poetry
* ethnic depth
* a vibrant festival atmosphere
